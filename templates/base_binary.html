About Troop 89

Troop 89 is a proud member of the Boy Scouts of America's Mayflower Council. We are chartered to American Legion Beckwith Post 110.

We hold weekly meetings at 7:00 PM at Dale Street School or Memorial School in Medfield. We also hold monthly overnight outings. See our calendar for detailed information on upcoming meetings events.

Contact the Troop

For more information about our troop, or for question regarding Troop 89's operations, please contact our scoutmaster.
